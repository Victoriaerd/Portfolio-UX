Heal's

Role

Creative Lead

Challenge

To improve customer experience when buying upholstery furniture.

Upholstery products can be bought in a range of fabric a feet options. However, for online customers it was difficult to visualise selected finish due to the lack of visual represantation. The product was shown as a line drawing with a fabric swatch overlayed in the corner. We wanted to improve the presentation as Heal's was missing out on online sales.

Solution

Due to the high costs of production, it was not practical to photograph every single product in every size and all the available finishes. We have explored an option of recolouring photographs, however the results were not satisfactory, the textures of the materials were too complex and different from one another to achieve photorealistic results.

We got in touch with a few visualisation agencies and worked with them on creating 3D prototypes. We focused on high quality of finishes, photorealistic geometry as well as realistic lighting and shadows.

After the research conducted we selected a 3D rendering agency partner. They worked on the creation of photorealistic 360 renders of the products based on our photographs, product dimensions and feedback. The video here is showing the result of this visualisation. Customers can easily select desired finish of the fabrics and legs and zoom in to see the finish in detail.

Outcome

Implementing 360 renders onto Heal's website resulted in 20% increase in sales in upholstery and cabinet categories. There was also an increase in products sold in finishes that were presented as renders, comparing to when finishes were only shown as swatches.

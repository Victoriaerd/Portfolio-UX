Hi, I am a Multidisciplinary UX Designer who codes, with a background in Photography, Videography and Economics.

While being fascinated with all aspects of User Experience design, I am passionate about research, storytelling, and visual design. Accessibility and inclusive design are very important to me.

I have worked on projects that involved visual re-branding, 3D models and 360 CGI renders delivery and content creation for start-ups as well as established brands.

My current home is London, UK but I grew up in Russia.

I enjoy collaborating on initiatives that cross disciplinary boundaries. If you'd like to work with me, please get in touch.
